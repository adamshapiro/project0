Latte

A latte is a coffee drink made by adding steamed milk to shots of espresso. The milk should be lightly aerated as it is steamed to about 160°F, then added to the espresso immediately before the milk and foam have a chance to separate. If done correctly, the milk should increase in volume by about 20%, with a small amount of microfoam. This allows for creating beautiful latte art!
